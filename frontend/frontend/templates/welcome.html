A massively multiplayer game

Scrobble Exchange is an online multiplayer game where users can invest in a portfolio of artists and gain returns based on the performance of their portfolio. By signing in with a Last.fm account, users can buy and sell their favourite artists and compete with others to earn the most points, acquire trophies, and build the best portfolio.

To get started, you will need a Last.fm account. Click the button below to authorise your account with Scrobble Exchange (you will be prompted to create an account if you do not already have one).
